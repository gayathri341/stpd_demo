    <h1 id="healthDescription1">EAT MORE FRUITS AND VEGGIES</h1>

    <p id="healthParagraph1">A diet rich in vegetables and fruits can lower blood pressure, reduce the risk of heart disease and stroke, prevent some types of cancer, lower risk of eye and digestive problems, and have a positive effect upon blood sugar, which can help keep appetite in check.</p>




    <h1 id="healthDescription2">EXERCISE</h1>
    <img id="health2" src="https://img.freepik.com/premium-vector/people-performing-sports-activities_102902-2562.jpg?w=360" alt="">
    <p id="healthParagraph2">Exercise is a body activity that enhances or maintains physical fitness and overall health and wellness.It is performed for various reasons, to aid growth and improve strength, develop muscles and the cardiovascular system, hone athletic skills, weight loss or maintenance, improve health, or simply for enjoyment. Many individuals choose to exercise outdoors where they can congregate in groups, socialize, and improve well-being as well as mental health.</p>


    <h1 id="healthDescription3">EMOTIONAL HEALTH</h1>
    <img id="health3" src="https://www.psychologs.com/blogimage/50997_1630152511.jpg" alt="">
    <p id="healthParagraph3">Emotional health is about how we think and feel. It is about our sense of wellbeing, our ability to cope with life events and how we acknowledge our own emotions as well as those of others. It doesn't mean being happy all of the time.</p>
